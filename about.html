About CS Learning

The Site

CS Learning is a collection of standalone, interactive learning resources aimed primarily at students following the AQA A Level Computer Science course. The site also includes a handful of interactives for A Level Maths and Physics.

Every interactive is a self-contained HTML page — no logins, no tracking, no installation. Just open and use.

Contact

The site is designed and maintained by Paul Baker. You can connect with me on LinkedIn.

A Note on AI-Assisted Content

The interactives on this site are primarily produced with AI-assisted (vibe) coding. While effort is made to ensure accuracy, AI-generated content can contain mistakes — in the logic, the explanations, or the underlying coverage.

Found a Bug? Have a Suggestion?

Bug spots, improvements and suggestions are very welcome! Feel free to message me via LinkedIn — corrections and ideas from students and teachers help make these resources better for everyone.
